## Meta Notes for Filming

### Optimal Posting Strategy
- **Best times:** Weekday evenings (6–10 PM) and weekend mornings
- **Post frequency:** 2–3 videos per week for momentum
- **Evergreen + Seasonal:** Scripts 1, 3, 4, 6, 7 are evergreen. Scripts 2, 8, 9 perform well during "gaming fails" trends

### Required B-Roll/Assets
1. Gameplay footage at 1080p+ (if possible)
2. Character creation and progression clips
3. Economy/banking interface close-ups
4. Character portrait reactions (sad, happy, stressed)
5. UI overlays with key stats
6. Transition effects between weeks/seasons

### Low-Budget Filming Tips
- Record directly from phone screen (no emulator needed)
- Use free text-to-speech for voiceover (or Isac's own voice)
- Free editing software: CapCut (built-in effects, trending sounds)
- Trending sounds available free in CapCut/TikTok editor
- No need for on-camera appearance (UI + voiceover is sufficient)

### Engagement Hooks
- End each video with: "Try it free. Link in bio."
- Respond to comments with: "What would YOU do differently?"
- Pin top comments asking "What's your build?"
- Go live occasionally: "Let's speedrun together"

### CTA (Call-to-Action) Text Overlay
