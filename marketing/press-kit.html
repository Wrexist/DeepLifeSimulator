Game Overview

A Life Simulator Without Limits

Deep Life Simulator puts you in control of a life worth living. Make decisions that matter, build wealth through real economic mechanics, pursue careers, find love, and navigate the consequences of your choices. Every decision creates a branching narrative unique to your playthrough.

Unlike other life simulators, Deep Life features a sophisticated economic engine with real-world financial mechanics: loans with interest, property investment, business ownership, bankruptcy, and compound interest. Your financial decisions have authentic weight.

What Sets It Apart

Deep Life Simulator goes beyond casual life sims with sophisticated mechanics and strategic depth:

Authentic Economics

Unlike other life sims, Deep Life features real financial mechanics: compound interest, loan amortization, property valuation, and investment returns. Your financial decisions matter.

Prestige System

Endless replayability through prestige levels that unlock new starting conditions and special events, encouraging players to return for completely different experiences.

Content Creator Path

Unique career path not found in competitors. Build an audience as a streamer or YouTuber and generate passive income through content monetization.

Player Agency

Every meaningful choice creates branching narratives. No "correct" way to play—live as a criminal, corporate executive, artist, or anything in between.

Indie Excellence

Built by a solo developer with 10+ years of game development experience. Personal care and attention to detail in every system.

Cross-Platform

iOS now, Android coming soon. Cloud-safe integration planned to let players continue their stories across devices seamlessly.
