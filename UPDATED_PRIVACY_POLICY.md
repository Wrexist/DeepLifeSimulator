# Privacy Policy for DeepLife Sim

**Last updated:** October 20, 2025

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

* **Game Data**: Your game progress, statistics, achievements, save slots, and in-game preferences stored locally on your device
* **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, app version, and device identifiers
* **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app, features used, session duration, and gameplay patterns
* **Purchase Data**: In-app purchase transaction receipts and purchase history (processed securely through Apple)
* **Tracking Data** (Optional): With your explicit permission via App Tracking Transparency, we may collect data for personalized advertising

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

* **App Functionality**: To save your game progress locally, provide game features, and process in-app purchases securely
* **User Experience**: To improve app performance, fix bugs, and enhance gameplay features
* **Advertising** (If you grant permission): To show relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
* **Customer Support**: To respond to your questions, feedback, and technical issues
* **Legal Compliance**: To comply with applicable laws and protect our legal rights

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

* **Apple App Store**: For secure processing of in-app purchases and app distribution
* **Expo**: For app development, deployment, and over-the-air updates
* **Google AdMob** (Currently disabled in this version): For displaying advertisements (requires your consent via App Tracking Transparency when enabled)

### Important Note:
- We do NOT use Firebase or any other analytics services beyond Apple's built-in frameworks
- We do NOT transmit your game data to external servers unless you explicitly enable cloud save features
- All game progress is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage

## 4. Data Storage and Transmission

* **Local Storage**: All game data (progress, saves, statistics) is stored locally on your device
* **No Cloud Storage by Default**: Your game data stays on your device unless you explicitly enable cloud save features
* **In-App Purchases**: Transaction receipts are validated with Apple's servers but not stored on our servers
* **Data Deletion**: Deleting the app will permanently remove all locally stored game data

## 5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. 

We may share limited data with:

* **Apple Inc.**: For in-app purchase processing and app distribution
* **Expo**: For app deployment and crash reporting (minimal device/app information only)
* **Google AdMob** (when enabled): For serving advertisements if you grant tracking permission
* **Legal Authorities**: Only when required by law or to protect legal rights

## 6. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In compliance with Apple's requirements:

* We will request your permission before tracking your activity across other apps and websites
* You can grant or deny this permission when first launching the app
* Denying tracking permission does NOT affect core app functionality
* You can change your tracking preferences anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking
* Tracking is ONLY used for personalized advertising when AdMob is enabled

## 7. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

* **Encryption**: Sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard methods
* **Secure Transmission**: All network communications use secure HTTPS protocols
* **Local Security**: Game data relies on iOS device security features
* **No Passwords**: App does not require account creation or password storage
* **Purchase Security**: All transactions processed through Apple's secure payment system

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

* **Game Data**: Stored locally until you delete the app or manually delete save files
* **Purchase Receipts**: Retained by Apple for 7 years (for tax and legal compliance)
* **Crash Reports**: Up to 90 days for debugging purposes
* **Analytics Data**: Not collected in current version

## 9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

* **Access**: View all your game data within the app (Statistics, Progress screens)
* **Correct**: Modify your character name and game preferences in Settings
* **Delete**: Remove all game data by deleting the app or using in-app save slot deletion
* **Opt-Out of Tracking**: Deny or revoke tracking permission in iOS Settings
* **Opt-Out of Ads**: Purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase when available
* **Portability**: Export your game data (cloud save feature when enabled)
* **Contact Us**: Reach out with any privacy concerns at DeepLifeSimulator@gmail.com

## 10. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

* Our app is rated **17+** and is NOT intended for children under 13
* We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
* If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at DeepLifeSimulator@gmail.com
* Parents can use iOS parental controls to restrict app usage and in-app purchases

## 11. In-App Purchases

* All purchases are processed securely through Apple's In-App Purchase system
* We do NOT store your payment information
* We only receive transaction receipts from Apple (no credit card data)
* Purchases are tied to your Apple ID
* You can restore previous purchases using the "Restore Purchases" feature
* Refunds are handled by Apple according to their refund policy

## 12. Changes to This Policy

* We may update this privacy policy from time to time
* We will notify you of significant changes by updating the app and posting the new policy
* The "Last updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision
* Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy
* You can always access the latest policy at: https://deeplifesimulator.github.io/privacy-policy/

## 13. International Users

* This app is available worldwide through the Apple App Store
